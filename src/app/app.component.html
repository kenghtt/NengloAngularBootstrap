In 1931, Nenglo Yang was born in Nameng, Laos to Paying and mother’s name. He also had an older sister named MyKer. At the age of 6, his mother passed away due to an ilness. Nenglo’s Father remarried sometimes later and had two sons. At the age of 15, Nenglo married Mai Joua Lee. Nenglo did not start his formal education until after he had married. Though he only attended school for a year, he quit in order to help his family with farming, and along with the marriage of his older sister.. At this time, the French Army had already colonized the nation, and due to the heavy government taxation, joining the French Military was the only way Nenglo could escape taxation without penalty.
